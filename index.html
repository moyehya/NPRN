About Us

At NPRN we believe Intelligence is Easy!

Established in 2021 and based out of Chennai, India. NPRN Innovations is specialized in wireless security and home automation domain. Within a year of establishment we have fostered partnership with companies from across the globe to bring you the best products in a economically viable way. Within a short span we've rapidly expanded our service and business which was possible only by adopting a customer first approach.

Our clientele includes large multinational firms with global footprints and we've been a part of prestigious governmental projects. One such project we're part of is the upgradation and development of legacy security systems to state of the art smart security surveillance in ATMs of major private and nationalised banks across India.
